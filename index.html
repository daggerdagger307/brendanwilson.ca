Hello! As I’m sure you know by now my name is Brendan Wilson, but what you don’t know just yet is that I’m addicted to design. Always starting with a pencil and paper while looking for that idea or hook if you will, I prefer to work towards a goal not click away and hope something finds me. Building, testing and sketching are the foundations to my ideas, which have proven in the past to be my biggest assets. Please feel free to contact me, even if you just want to talk widows!
