Brendan Wilson

Hello! As I’m sure you know by now my name is Brendan Wilson, but what you don’t know just yet is that I’m addicted to design. It overwhelms my every thought and usually finds its way into my casual conversation in no time at all. Sometimes much to the chagrin of the person next to me on the bus, who probably doesn’t care that the bus add above us is littered with widows (the typography kind.) Despite my annoyed fellow travellers, I have pursued a career in design, in one form or another, for the better part of my life, and I have come to call this pursuit Hands On Design. I am a designer who would prefer not hash out a project hunched over a keyboard or swirling a mouse. I prefer to get my hand dirty so to speak. Always starting with a pencil and paper while looking for that idea of hook if you will, I prefer to work towards a goal not click away and hope something finds me. Building, testing and sketching are the foundations to my ideas, which have proven in the past to be my biggest assets. Please feel free to contact me, even if you just want to talk widows!
